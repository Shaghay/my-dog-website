Baloot

My name is Baloot. I was born in Iran in a cold day of december 2020. I'm probably a mixed of two races; Shih-Tzu and Terrier.
I was born with dark brown and black fur, but turned to gray and white!!!

Since summer 2021 I live in Germany, but still feel homesick.
My best friend here is Mascha from Pague! We play together two to three times per week.
